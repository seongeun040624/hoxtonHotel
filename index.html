The Hoxton Dublin, Now Open

Tucked away on bustling Exchequer Street in a historic building, our Dublin home puts you right in the heart of the city’s cultural core. Think cobbled lanes, artisan coffee shops, hidden bars, and some of the best bites in the city.

Love is in the air

Valentine's Day, Pal-entine's Day... we're total lovebirds at The Hox, so how about heading our way to share the love? Check-in and cosy up with your loved one(s).
